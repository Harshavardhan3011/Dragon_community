## 1. Requirement Counts & Math Formulas

To avoid rounding discrepancies, progress is calculated using two methods:
1.  **Direct Status Rates**: The exact ratio of requirements in each state (Completed, Partial, Missing) relative to the total.
2.  **Weighted Progress**: Evaluated on a scale of `1.0` for Completed, `0.5` for Partial, and `0.0` for Missing.

$$Weighted\ Progress\ \% = \frac{Completed + (Partial \times 0.5)}{Total} \times 100\%$$
